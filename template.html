Spark Excitement with Animated Party Invitations


Don't just invite guests. Delight guests with invitations that set the scene for your best party yet. Every design in our diverse invitation collection is carefully crafted by a team of animators, designers and writers, all with the goal of helping you get guests excited about the fun to come.


Animation adds energy and serves to unveil your party details with a flourish, building a special feeling of celebration. Tons of personalization options, including custom music, favorite photos, text and creative touches, let you create a stand-out invite that reflects the unique spirit of your event. Your guest of honor is sure to feel truly celebrated thanks to the many thoughtful details.


Find your dream theme in our huge variety of invitations for every occasion big or small, from a twins baby shower or breakfast with Santa, to a cocktail soiree or a surprise 50th birthday party. Once you've personalized and emailed your just-right invitation, our RSVP feature helpfully tracks guests' replies, for easier party planning.
